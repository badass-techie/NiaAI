To promote and protect democracy by educating citizens on their rights and responsibilities, and advocating for transparent and accountable governance at all levels.
To increase civic engagement and participation through community organizing, voter education, and mobilization efforts.
To promote equality and social justice by fighting against voter suppression and ensuring that all eligible citizens have access to the ballot.
To promote international cooperation and diplomacy to strengthen democratic institutions and support peaceful democratic transitions in emerging democracies.
To support the development of independent media and free press, as an essential component of a healthy democracy, by advocating for press freedom and media literacy.

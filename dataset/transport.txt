To promote sustainable transport solutions by advocating for the development of public transportation systems that reduce carbon emissions and increase accessibility.
To create a safer transport environment for all road users by advocating for and enforcing road safety laws and regulations.
To increase access to transport for underprivileged communities by providing affordable and reliable public transportation options in areas where private transportation is not viable.
To promote the use of active transport modes such as cycling and walking by developing infrastructure that supports and encourages these modes of transport.
To reduce the environmental impact of the transport sector by encouraging the use of low-emission transportation modes and supporting the development of alternative fuels and energy sources.
